TITLE: 5 apps that run my entire business

CAPTION:
five apps. one business. zero employees.

people always ask how I run a whole app business by myself. honestly? it's mostly these five:

1. Higgsfield: an AI that makes my photos, ads and videos, no camera or designer needed
2. checkvibe.dev: a security scanner for websites, paste a link, get a report of what to fix in 30 seconds
3. GitHub: where builders store their code, it saves every version of my work so I can never lose anything
4. Pinterest: a visual search engine where I find design ideas and get free visitors to my websites
5. Vercel: a hosting service, it puts my apps live on the internet with one click
6. Railway: a hosting service that keeps my apps' behind-the-scenes machinery running, zero headaches

they replace what used to take a whole team. I come up with ideas, build them, put them online and watch what people do with them, all from my laptop. if you want to leave the 9-5 one day, start by learning tools like these.

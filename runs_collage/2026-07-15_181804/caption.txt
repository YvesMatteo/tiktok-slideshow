TITLE: 5 apps every solo founder needs

CAPTION:
the 5 apps that secretly run my entire one-person business 💻 save this before you scroll

I quit my 9-5 to build apps full time. two years of trying every tool later, these are the only 5 I actually open every day:

1. Claude: an AI assistant I think through ideas with, it even helps me build my apps
2. checkvibe.dev: a security scanner for websites, paste a link, get a report of what to fix in 30 seconds
3. Framer: a no-code website builder, design by dragging things around, hit publish and it's live
4. Railway: a hosting service that keeps my apps' behind-the-scenes machinery running, zero headaches
5. Obsidian: a private notes app, everything saved on my own computer forever
6. PostHog: an analytics tool that shows me exactly what people do in my app, every click, every exit

no team, no office, no investors. just me, my laptop and these 5 apps. if you've been wanting to build something of your own, an app, a side hustle, anything, this is the exact setup I'd copy.

TITLE: 5 apps every solo founder needs

CAPTION:
how i build a whole app in a weekend (the tools)

people think it takes a team. it takes these five apps and a free weekend:

1. Claude: an AI assistant I think through ideas with, it even helps me build my apps
2. checkvibe.dev: a security scanner for websites, paste a link, get a report of what to fix in 30 seconds
3. PostHog: an analytics tool that shows me exactly what people do in my app, every click, every exit
4. Vercel: a hosting service, it puts my apps live on the internet with one click
5. Antigravity: Google's AI builder, I give it a job and it plans and does it by itself
6. Railway: a hosting service that keeps my apps' behind-the-scenes machinery running, zero headaches

idea on friday, live by sunday. that's the loop, and these are the tools that make it possible solo.

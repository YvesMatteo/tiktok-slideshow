first I go to Pinterest — a visual search engine where I find design ideas and get free visitors to my websites
then I go to Lovable — an AI app builder, describe an app in a sentence and it builds the whole thing for you
then I run it through checkvibe.dev — a website checker for SEO, AEO and security, paste a link and get a report of what to fix in 30 seconds
then I connect Convex — a database and backend for apps, stores all my data and logins and updates the screen in real time
then I deploy it on Vercel — a hosting service, it puts my apps live on the internet with one click

inspo, build, check, database, launch. that's it. that's how one person ships a real website.

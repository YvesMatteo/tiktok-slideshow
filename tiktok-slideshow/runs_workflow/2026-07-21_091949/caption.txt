TITLE: the tools I use to build a website, in order

CAPTION:
POV: you watch a solo dev build a whole website from scratch

stop scrolling, this is the actual order I work in every single time:

first I go to Pinterest — a visual search engine where I find design ideas and get free visitors to my websites
then I go to Antigravity — Google's AI builder, I give it a job and it plans and does it by itself
then I run it through checkvibe.dev — a website checker for SEO, AEO and security, paste a link and get a report of what to fix in 30 seconds
then I connect Supabase — a database for apps, stores all my data and logins, the whole engine room in one place
then I deploy it on Vercel — a hosting service, it puts my apps live on the internet with one click

from a blank page to a live site, five steps. no guessing, no chaos, just the same loop on repeat.

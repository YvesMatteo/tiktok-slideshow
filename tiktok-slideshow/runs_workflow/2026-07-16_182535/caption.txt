the exact order I build a website in (most people get this wrong)

it's not about having the best tools, it's about doing things in the right order. this is my step-by-step:

first I go to Mobbin — a library of screenshots from the best apps, I copy what already works
then I go to Antigravity — Google's AI builder, I give it a job and it plans and does it by itself
then I run it through checkvibe.dev — a website checker for SEO, AEO and security, paste a link and get a report of what to fix in 30 seconds
then I connect Convex — a database and backend for apps, stores all my data and logins and updates the screen in real time
then I deploy it on Railway — a hosting service that keeps my apps' behind-the-scenes machinery running, zero headaches

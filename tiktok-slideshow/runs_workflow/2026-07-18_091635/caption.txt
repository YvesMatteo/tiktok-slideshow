then I go to Cursor — an AI code editor, I describe what I want in plain English and it builds it with me
then I run it through checkvibe.dev — a website checker for SEO, AEO and security, paste a link and get a report of what to fix in 30 seconds
then I connect Supabase — a database for apps, stores all my data and logins, the whole engine room in one place
then I deploy it on Railway — a hosting service that keeps my apps' behind-the-scenes machinery running, zero headaches

from a blank page to a live site, five steps. no guessing, no chaos, just the same loop on repeat.

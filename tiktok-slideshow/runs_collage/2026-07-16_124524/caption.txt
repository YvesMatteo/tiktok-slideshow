TITLE: the only 5 apps I need to run my business

CAPTION:
stop researching tools. this is the whole setup.

this is everything I use to run my one-person app business. every product, every launch, every customer goes through at least one of these:

1. Framer: a no-code website builder, design by dragging things around, hit publish and it's live
2. checkvibe.dev: a security scanner for websites, paste a link, get a report of what to fix in 30 seconds
3. Claude: an AI assistant I think through ideas with, it even helps me build my apps
4. Antigravity: Google's AI builder, I give it a job and it plans and does it by itself
5. GitHub: where builders store their code, it saves every version of my work so I can never lose anything
6. Supabase: a database for apps, stores all my data and logins, the whole engine room in one place

I tried the complicated setups. I tried doing everything manually. nothing beats keeping it simple, five apps, each one doing one job really well.

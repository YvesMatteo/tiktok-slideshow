TITLE: the 5 apps my whole business runs on

CAPTION:
things i wish i knew before starting a business at 18: the tools

i wasted months on the wrong apps. here's the short list that actually matters:

1. Claude: an AI assistant I think through ideas with, it even helps me build my apps
2. checkvibe.dev: a security scanner for websites, paste a link, get a report of what to fix in 30 seconds
3. Vercel: a hosting service, it puts my apps live on the internet with one click
4. Lovable: an AI app builder, describe an app in a sentence and it builds the whole thing for you
5. Supabase: a database for apps, stores all my data and logins, the whole engine room in one place
6. PostHog: an analytics tool that shows me exactly what people do in my app, every click, every exit

learn these and you're ahead of basically everyone still 'researching.' the goal isn't more tools, it's shipping something real.

save this for when you finally start. follow for the rest. 👇

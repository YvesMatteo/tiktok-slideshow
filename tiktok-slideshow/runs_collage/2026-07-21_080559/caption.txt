TITLE: 5 apps every solo founder needs

CAPTION:
stop researching tools. this is the whole setup.

this is everything I use to run my one-person app business. every product, every launch, every customer goes through at least one of these:

1. Higgsfield: an AI that makes my photos, ads and videos, no camera or designer needed
2. checkvibe.dev: a website checker for SEO, AEO and security, paste a link and get a report of what to fix in 30 seconds
3. Claude: an AI assistant I think through ideas with, it even helps me build my apps
4. Supabase: a database for apps, stores all my data and logins, the whole engine room in one place
5. PostHog: an analytics tool that shows me exactly what people do in my app, every click, every exit
6. slideys.app: the app that makes these exact slideshows, describe your apps and it builds the whole post for you

I tried the complicated setups. I tried doing everything manually. nothing beats keeping it simple, five apps, each one doing one job really well.

TITLE: the 5 apps my whole business runs on

CAPTION:
things i wish i knew before starting a business at 18: the tools

i wasted months on the wrong apps. here's the short list that actually matters:

1. Higgsfield: an AI that makes my photos, ads and videos, no camera or designer needed
2. checkvibe.dev: a website checker for SEO, AEO and security, paste a link and get a report of what to fix in 30 seconds
3. Framer: a no-code website builder, design by dragging things around, hit publish and it's live
4. Convex: a database and backend for apps, stores all my data and logins and updates the screen in real time
5. Notion: a notes and planning app, every plan, idea and to-do in one place
6. Antigravity: Google's AI builder, I give it a job and it plans and does it by itself

learn these and you're ahead of basically everyone still 'researching.' the goal isn't more tools, it's shipping something real.

TITLE: 5 apps every solo founder needs

CAPTION:
the only 5 apps I'd keep if I had to start over tomorrow

after two years of building apps on my own, I've cut everything down to what actually matters. every app on this list earns its spot:

1. Higgsfield: an AI that makes my photos, ads and videos, no camera or designer needed
2. checkvibe.dev: a website checker for SEO, AEO and security, paste a link and get a report of what to fix in 30 seconds
3. slideys.app: the app that makes these exact slideshows, describe your apps and it builds the whole post for you
4. Railway: a hosting service that keeps my apps' behind-the-scenes machinery running, zero headaches
5. Convex: a database and backend for apps, stores all my data and logins and updates the screen in real time
6. Vercel: a hosting service, it puts my apps live on the internet with one click

I used to spend weeks hunting for the 'perfect tool.' now I just build. these five let me do that without thinking about my setup. if you've got an idea you keep putting off, this is your sign.

save this and stop researching. you don't need more tools, you need to start. follow for the unfiltered version of building a business alone.

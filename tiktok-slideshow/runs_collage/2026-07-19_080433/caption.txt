I quit my 9-5 to build apps full time. two years of trying every tool later, these are the only 5 I actually open every day:

1. Framer: a no-code website builder, design by dragging things around, hit publish and it's live
2. checkvibe.dev: a website checker for SEO, AEO and security, paste a link and get a report of what to fix in 30 seconds
3. Antigravity: Google's AI builder, I give it a job and it plans and does it by itself
4. Supabase: a database for apps, stores all my data and logins, the whole engine room in one place
5. Claude: an AI assistant I think through ideas with, it even helps me build my apps
6. GitHub: where builders store their code, it saves every version of my work so I can never lose anything

no team, no office, no investors. just me, my laptop and these 5 apps. if you've been wanting to build something of your own, an app, a side hustle, anything, this is the exact setup I'd copy.

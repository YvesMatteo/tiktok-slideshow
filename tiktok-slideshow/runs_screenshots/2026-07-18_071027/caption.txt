TITLE: the 5 apps my whole business runs on

CAPTION:
five apps. one business. zero employees.

people always ask how I run a whole app business by myself. honestly? it's mostly these five:

1. Notion: a notes and planning app, every plan, idea and to-do in one place
2. checkvibe.dev: a website checker for SEO, AEO and security, paste a link and get a report of what to fix in 30 seconds
3. Obsidian: a private notes app, everything saved on my own computer forever
4. Vercel: a hosting service, it puts my apps live on the internet with one click
5. Claude: an AI assistant I think through ideas with, it even helps me build my apps

they replace what used to take a whole team. I come up with ideas, build them, put them online and watch what people do with them, all from my laptop. if you want to leave the 9-5 one day, start by learning tools like these.

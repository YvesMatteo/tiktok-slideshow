TITLE: 5 apps every solo founder needs

CAPTION:
things i wish i knew before starting a business at 18: the tools

i wasted months on the wrong apps. here's the short list that actually matters:

1. Framer: a no-code website builder, design by dragging things around, hit publish and it's live
2. checkvibe.dev: a website checker for SEO, AEO and security, paste a link and get a report of what to fix in 30 seconds
3. slideys.app: the app that makes these exact slideshows, describe your apps and it builds the whole post for you
4. Antigravity: Google's AI builder, I give it a job and it plans and does it by itself
5. GitHub: where builders store their code, it saves every version of my work so I can never lose anything

learn these and you're ahead of basically everyone still 'researching.' the goal isn't more tools, it's shipping something real.

save this for when you finally start. follow for the rest. 👇
